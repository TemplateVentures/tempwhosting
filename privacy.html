Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle information when you use our domain analysis tool (the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

When you use our tool, we may collect the following information based on the domain you enter:

  • IP address of the domain
  • Hosting provider
  • Server location
  • WHOIS data
  • Name server (DNS) records

We do not collect, log, or store any personally identifiable information about you as a user. The domain-related data retrieved is based on publicly accessible records.

2. How We Use the Information

The data is used solely to:

  • Display technical information about the domain you enter
  • Help users analyze website hosting and DNS setups

We do not sell, share, or retain any of the data provided.

3. Third-Party Services

Our tool uses the following external services:

  • ipinfo.io API for IP geolocation
  • Shell utilities or system functions (e.g., whois, dns_get_record) to retrieve domain data

These services may collect or log usage data per their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their respective policies.

4. Cookies

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies.

5. Data Security

Since we do not store user data, no personal data is at risk. However, we use secure server environments to ensure tool integrity.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
